Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Compass ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. What We Collect

When you sign up for early access, we collect:

  • Your email address

We do not collect any other personal information at this time.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your email address is used solely to:

  • Add you to the Compass early access waitlist
  • Send you updates about the product launch and related news

We will never sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Formspree to process form submissions. Your email address is stored securely through their platform. You can review Formspree's privacy policy at formspree.io.

4. Data Retention

We retain your email address only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or until you request removal.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Unsubscribe from communications at any time using the link in any email we send
  • Request that your data be deleted by contacting us directly
  • Ask what personal data we hold about you

6. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact the Compass team through the Mindset Lab, EMBA 8025, University of Fredericton.

7. Disclaimer

Compass is not a real product launch. This site was created as part of an innovation course project (Mindset Lab, EMBA 8025). Data collected is used solely for academic research purposes.
